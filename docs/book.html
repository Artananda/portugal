The Art of Living and Loving

This book is about the life of an Olaf who was torn from life at the age of 49 and born again as a new being with the experience from his past life. This book outlines the transformation process that he has been going through since that day in January 2014.
Then he learns about orgasms, after his heart opening, during a tantra seminar, that are rather untypical for men.
He more or less gets out of the capitalistic system and lives in his mobile home in the middle of Berlin for some years, moved to Portugal and lives in a rainbow community, does yoga every day and touches other people in a special way, healing kind.
He sees himself as a kind of enlightened man, because through all these transformations he has experienced the unconditional love that he is able to give himself and others.
Today he calls himself Ananda (absence of misfortune) and also Art (warrior of light), depending on which role he is currently playing in the role play called reality. The name Adam stands for the only one man on earth from the perspective of the garden Eden mentioned in the bible, in respect that all men and women are ONE.
We hope that you can take something from this book with you personally and if not, it doesn't matter.
