What is the Prana-Flow Ritual?

The Prana-Flow Ritual is based on the tantric Maithuna ritual, where Shiva and Shakti unite. This is essentially a couple's massage, an adaptation of the Maithuna ritual, where the energies are directed and released through specific massage techniques, which can lead to an elevated energetic state.

During the ritual, both partners, as in the Maithuna ritual, may sit in the Yub-Yum position and massage each other. While a physical union of the partners can take place, it is not the goal of this ritual. The primary aim is to activate the flow of Kundalini energy through mutual massage.

Partners alternate, with one lying down from the Yub-Yum position to receive a massage from the other. Afterward, they return to the sitting position, remaining connected with their partner. It is not necessary for a physical union to occur; simply having the Lingam near the Yoni, while the partners touch each other, is sufficient to allow energy to flow. An erection of the Lingam or a lubricated Yoni is not required.

It is important to emphasize that this ritual is not focused on sexual gratification but rather on energy work and spiritual healing. As the facilitator, I merely guide the process, while the couples experience the ritual together.
